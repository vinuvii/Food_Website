Tiramisu

One of Italy’s most popular desserts, tiramisu is an elegant, rich layering of bold espresso and cocoa, creamy mascarpone, sweet Marsala wine, and delicate ladyfingers, a low-density sponge cake-like cookie. The ladyfingers, which are themselves classic Italian treats, are briefly soaked in an espresso and sugar mixture to soften them.

Torta Caprese Recipe

This delicious flourless chocolate cake is full of dark chocolate and almonds and originated on the island of Capri in Italy. The island is famous for its bright yellow lemon liqueur, Limoncello, and while some recipes call for Rum or Gran Marnie, we give this flourless chocolate torte all 5 stars when made with the original lemony goodness.
